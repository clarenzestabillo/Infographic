Quick Breakdown

Volleyball is a team sport played between two teams of six players each, separated by a net. The objective is to send the ball over the net and ground it on the opponent's side while preventing the same effort by the opposing team. Players use a combination of serving, passing, setting, attacking, blocking, and digging to score points.

Steps to Execute Key Volleyball Tricks

  1. The Serve
    • Underhand Serve: Hold the ball... strike with the heel of your hand.
    • Overhand Serve: Toss high... strike with open hand.
  2. Bump (Forearm Pass)
    • Form a platform with forearms.
    • Use legs to direct the ball.
  3. Set (Overhead Pass)
    • Hands in diamond above forehead.
    • Use fingertips to push the ball up.
  4. Spike (Attack Hit)
    • Quick approach, jump, and swing arm downward.
  5. Block (Defensive Move)
    • Jump with arms up to stop attack.

History of Volleyball

Volleyball was invented in 1895 by William G. Morgan, a physical education instructor in Holyoke, Massachusetts, USA. Originally called "Mintonette," the game was designed as a less intense alternative to basketball, combining elements of tennis, handball, and baseball. The first official match was played at the Springfield YMCA, and the name was later changed to "volleyball" due to the volleying nature of the game.
